---
title: "Free-Viewpoint Video"
weight: 1
summary: "Capturing large-scale events with many cameras to create 3D video media in which the viewer chooses the viewpoint."
---

By capturing events held in large-scale spaces — such as soccer matches or concerts — with many video cameras and processing the footage, we realize 3D video media in which the choice of viewpoint belongs to the viewer. This technology holds promise for applications in entertainment and sports science.
