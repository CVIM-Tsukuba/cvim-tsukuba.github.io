Our current research topics include free-viewpoint video media (realizing 3D television), massive sensing (providing safe and secure living spaces), mixed-reality information presentation (the evolution of virtual reality), and surveillance systems that combine environmental and mobile cameras.

Videos related to our research and laboratory are collected on the "[Videos](/en/videos)" page — please take a look there as well.
