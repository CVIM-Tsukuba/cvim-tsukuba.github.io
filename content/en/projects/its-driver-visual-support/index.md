---
title: "Driver Visual Support in Intelligent Transport Systems (ITS)"
weight: 3
summary: "Using road surveillance cameras to capture information in the driver's blind spots and present it in an easy-to-understand form."
---

Using road surveillance cameras, we acquire information from the driver's blind spots, apply image processing, and provide it to the driver in an easy-to-understand form. In this way we develop systems that support the driver's visual awareness of the surrounding situation.
