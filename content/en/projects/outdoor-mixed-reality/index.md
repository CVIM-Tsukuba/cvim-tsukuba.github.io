---
title: "Outdoor Mixed Reality"
weight: 4
summary: "Bringing mixed reality outdoors, fusing information unavailable in the real world alone to support pedestrians' visual awareness."
---

We are advancing research on using mixed-reality technology outdoors. By fusing a variety of information that cannot be obtained from the real world alone with the real world and presenting it, we can provide visual support for outdoor pedestrians, among other things.
