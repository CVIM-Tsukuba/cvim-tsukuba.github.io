## As a graduate student

Please apply to one of the following programs, which our faculty supervise:

- Master's/Doctoral Program in Intelligent and Mechanical Interaction Systems, Degree Programs in Systems and Information Engineering, University of Tsukuba
- Ph.D. Program in Empowerment Informatics, Degree Programs in Systems and Information Engineering, University of Tsukuba
- Ph.D. Program in Humanics, Global Education Office, University of Tsukuba

See the details of each program below:

- IMIS: [Program in Intelligent and Mechanical Interaction Systems](https://www.imis.tsukuba.ac.jp/)
- EMP: [Program in Empowerment Informatics](https://www.emp.tsukuba.ac.jp/)
- HX: [Program in Humanics](https://www.phd-humanics.tsukuba.ac.jp/)

Before applying, please contact one of Kameda, Kitahara, or Tsukada (each faculty member supervises different research topics; information on these topics is also available on the [introduction video](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCLh7dSlxJdk2VYkRXO0iWEw/videos) page).

## As an undergraduate

Become a student of the College of Engineering Systems at the University of Tsukuba — either by entering in the first year or transferring into the third year — and choose our laboratory for your graduation research.

Every summer, an information session for high-school students is held by the College of Engineering Systems. For details, please see [here](https://ac.tsukuba.ac.jp/opencampus).

## As a researcher

For joint research, please contact Kameda or Kitahara.

Apart from the above, we welcome laboratory visits at any time. If you are interested, please feel free to come to Room 3M310 or the 3rd floor of the Center for Computational Sciences (please contact us in advance).
