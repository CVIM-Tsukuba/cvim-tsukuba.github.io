**305-8577**
1-1-1 Tennodai, Tsukuba, Ibaraki, Japan
Computer Vision and Image Media Lab., 3rd floor, Center for Computational Sciences (CCS), University of Tsukuba

### From Tsukuba Center to the campus

Buses on the University of Tsukuba loop line depart from bus stop No. 6 at Tsukuba Center (roughly every 20 minutes, 260 yen).

- For Room 3M310, the nearest stop is the 10th stop, "Daisan Area-mae" (about 10 minutes).
- For the 3rd floor of CCS (Center for Computational Sciences), the nearest stop is the 9th stop, "Daiichi Area-mae" (about 9 minutes).

### To Tsukuba Center

[Transportation access and route map to the Tsukuba campus](http://www.tsukuba.ac.jp/access/tsukuba_access.html)

From Akihabara, the Tsukuba Express (TX) rapid service reaches Tsukuba Station (station No. 20) in 45 minutes. From there, take the Kanto Railway university loop bus (clockwise) and get off at "Daisan Area-mae."

Reference: [Transportation access to the Center for Computational Sciences](https://www.ccs.tsukuba.ac.jp/access/)
